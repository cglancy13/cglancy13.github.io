Why This Book?

Family dynamics can be confusing for children. A child is learning what is considered "good" behavior and what is not. They are taught that evil exists but don't quite know what it looks like. Sometimes children assume that something unfamiliar must be bad. This story explores the idea that people are not monsters, but they may have monsters that need to be conquered.

Message 1
Making the most of a bad situation has been a problem solver for many years. Many of us have heard the phrase, "When life hands you lemons, make lemonade." A positive attitude often yeilds the best results.
Message 2
People and amimals are not evil. The acts they perform can be evil, but that does not remove the dignity of the individual.
Message 3
We are often afraid of the unfamiliar. Someone from a small town dreads going into a big city. The more education and life experiences we have, the more we come to understand and accept one another.
